I used to get my 2%

Have you stopped getting your two percent? Do you find it hard to maintain 2% physical activity in your daily life? You are not alone—many people find it difficult to consistently find the time to exercise every day. Focus on the positive aspect of your new goal, rather than previous problems. Every day that you exercise is a step towards long-term health.

How about developing a tailored strategy designed for your daily physical activity? To be specific, you can adjust your daily physical activities by reorganizing 2% of your day. Also, you can ask your family or friends to give you a kind of reward be a part of your reward when you achieve your daily goal. Thus, don’t be afraid of restarting in getting your two percent. Let’s make an attempt with more specific strategies in order to make a real success of keeping your 2% physical activity. Are you ready to achieve a healthier lifestyle?
