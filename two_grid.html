About The Site

This Website is a tool to help you get your daily 2%. We realize that everyone has different exercise goals, and our goal is to tailor yourtwopercent.org to help you meet your goal.

The CGU team would like to acknowledge Dr. BJ Fogg of Stanford University, who designed the behavior change pathways that we used in the design of our site. Check out his work at http://www.behaviorgrid.org/
