I will get more than 2% for two weeks!

You are now realizing the benefits of having your 2% and you also see an opportunity to get a bit more in your schedule. Try it out—for two weeks, turn 2% into 3% (45 minutes daily) or even 4% (1 hour daily). But watch out! You may become addicted!

  • Try adding on a little bit to your existing workout. Walk an extra few blocks, stretch after your workout, or try some strength training.
  • This is a great time to try out something new! Always wanted to check out swing dancing, but haven’t gotten around to it? This is your chance!
  • Keep track of your progress. At the end of the two weeks, reward yourself! Try out a new healthy restaurant, purchase a small treat (new exercise gear, anyone?), or just make time for a hike with a friend.
