What's YOUR two percent?

What’s Your Two Percent? is a movement about movement. Exercising for thirty minutes, or two percent of a 24-hour day, contributes to better health and a more positive lifestyle. The CGU research team encourages you to “get your two percent” by providing information and suggestions tailored specifically to your exercise levels and interests. Click on a link below to get started!

What does 2% mean?

I Want to Get My 2%

I don't get my 2% every day, but I would like to start.

I Used To Get My 2%

I'd like to start getting my 2% again. I know it will make me feel happier and healthier.

I Am Getting My 2%

I would like to maintain my current level of activity or increase my efforts!
